üó∫Ô∏è Discovery

Rarity & Location

  • Extremely Rare: Only 0.05% chance per chunk (approximately 1 in 2000 chunks)
  • Distance-Gated: Only generate 1000+ blocks from world spawn
  • Buried Deep: Structures are located 10-25 blocks below surface level
  • All Biomes: Can generate in any biome, with biome influencing ruin type

üîç Finding Ruins

  • Surface Signs: Look for unusual terrain patterns or slight depressions
  • Cave Systems: Sometimes connect to nearby cave networks
  • Mining: Most commonly discovered while mining or exploring underground
  • Patience Required: Due to extreme rarity, finding ruins requires extensive exploration

üõê Temple Ruins

Religious and ceremonial structures with spiritual themes.

Features

  • Central Chamber: Large 7x7 ceremonial hall with altar
  • Side Chambers: Four smaller rooms for prayer and storage
  • Connecting Corridors: Ancient hallways linking all areas
  • Ceremonial Altar: Central enchanting table with rare block displays
  • Atmospheric Lighting: Torch-lit ambiance throughout

NPCs & Trading

  • Cleric Villagers: 2 clerics for trading healing items and experience
  • Trading: Emeralds, bottles o' enchanting, healing potions

Loot

  • Treasure Chests: Religious items and emeralds

üìö Library Ruins

Centers of ancient knowledge and magical learning.

  • Study Chambers: Private reading and research areas
  • Enchanting Area: Central enchanting table with lecterns
  • Book Storage: Damaged but functional bookshelf walls
  • Reading Spaces: Furnished areas for scholarly pursuits

NPCs & Trading

  • Librarian Villagers: 2 librarians for book and enchantment trading
  • Trading: Enchanted books, emeralds, paper, books

Loot

  • Knowledge Storage: Chests containing books and written materials
  • Magical Items: Enchanting-related materials and rare books

Common Biomes: Jungle, Forest, Plains, Meadow

üéÆ Gameplay Tips

Exploration Strategy

  1. Go Far: Travel at least 1000 blocks from spawn before searching
  2. Search Systematically: Explore underground thoroughly in promising areas
  3. Look for Patterns: Unusual underground spaces might indicate nearby ruins
  4. Be Patient: Ruins are extremely rare - persistence is key

Preparation

  • Mining Gear: Bring pickaxes, torches, and food for extended underground exploration
  • Trading Materials: Carry emeralds and tradeable items for villager interactions
  • Storage Space: Bring extra inventory space for loot collection
  • Safety Equipment: Armor and weapons for underground hazards

üí∞ Villager Trading

  • Unique Professions: Each ruin type has specific villager professions
  • Renewable Trading: Villagers restock regularly like normal villagers
  • Underground Commerce: Trade in atmospheric underground settings
  • Specialized Goods: Access to profession-specific items and materials

‚öôÔ∏è Technical Details

Structure Generation

Ancient Ruins structures are generated underground based on the following parameters:

structures: # Ancient ruins (buried underground structures with villagers) ancient-ruins-chance: 0.0005 # Extremely rare - about 1 in 2000 chunks (0.05%) ancient-ruins-min-distance: 1000.0 # Minimum distance from spawn before ruins can generate

Configuration Options

  • ancient-ruins-chance: Probability of generation per chunk (default: 0.0005)
  • ancient-ruins-min-distance: Minimum distance from spawn in blocks (default: 1000.0)

Adjustment Guidelines

  • Increase Chance: Values up to 0.002 (0.2%) for more frequent discoveries
  • Decrease Distance: Minimum 500 blocks recommended to maintain exploration value
  • Server Performance: Higher generation rates may impact performance
