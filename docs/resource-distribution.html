üìä Resource Overview

‚úÖ Recent Ore Distribution Improvements

The ore generation system has been completely rebalanced to address clustering issues and improve resource availability for crafting and building.

  • Distributed Ore Generation: Each ore type uses separate noise functions to prevent massive clusters
  • Reduced Chaos Bonuses: Chaos effects on ore generation have been significantly reduced
  • Enhanced Building Materials: Added clay, sand, and gravel generation for crafting needs
  • Improved Wood Generation: Doubled tree generation chance from 10% to 20%

üîß Building Material Enhancements

New building materials have been strategically placed throughout the world:

  • Clay Generation: Available in deep areas (Y < 40) with 30% chance in suitable locations
  • Sand Availability: Now generates in transition zones (Y 40-60) for glass crafting
  • Gravel Deposits: Common in deep and transition areas for concrete production
  • Enhanced Wood: Tree generation doubled from 10% to 20% base chance
  • Stone Variety: Clumped generation of granite, diorite, andesite, tuff, and calcite

Before: chaosBonus = context.chaosIntensity * 0.4 (40% bonus)

Building Material Integration

  • Sand: Available in transition zones for glass making
  • Gravel: Common in multiple depth layers

Recommended Adjustments

  • More Resources: Decrease ore thresholds by 0.05-0.1
  • Less Chaos: Increase chaos thresholds to 0.9-1.0
  • More Wood: Increase tree-generation-chance to 0.3-0.4
  • Balanced Mining: Keep current settings for intended challenge

üéÆ Gameplay Balance

The resource distribution is designed to support all vanilla Minecraft crafting recipes while maintaining the chaotic, challenging nature of the Far Lands. Players should be able to:

  • Craft all basic tools and equipment with common ores
  • Build structures with varied and interesting materials
  • Create glass, bricks, and concrete for advanced builds
  • Progress through all game stages from wood to netherite
  • Experience unique Far Lands materials not found in vanilla
